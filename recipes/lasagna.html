Classic Lasagna

Classic lasagna is a dish you never get tired of, right? With this recipe,
your lasagna will be perfect with a soft and lovely texture and it also has
a sweet and exemplary taste. The parmesan cheese is the icing on the cake!

    Ingredients
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 4 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 390 g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 meat bouillon cube
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 9 dried lasagna sheets (cooking time 20 minutes)

    • Sauce
  • 6 tbsp butter (6 tablespoons corresponds to approx. 90 g)
  • 6 tbsp flour
  • 10 dl milk
  • 2 dl grated Parmesan

    • For serving
  • salad
    Do this
  1. Set the oven to 175Â°C.
  2. Sauce: Melt the butter and stir in the flour. Dilute with the milk while
    whisking. Let the sauce boil for about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Alternate the sauce, lasagne plates and the minced meat sauce in an oven-safe
    form (approx. 20 x 30 cm). Finish with sauce and cheese.
  4. Put the lasagna in the middle of the oven for about 40 minutes.
  5. To serve: Serve the lasagna with salad.
